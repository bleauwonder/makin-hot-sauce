Crystals Collector!

How to Play

At the start of the game, the Computer will choose a random number.

There are 4 crystals below. Each one will be assigned a certain random number that stays the same for the round. Each one you click will add to the total of your crystal power.

To win, match your total with the Computer's total. To lose, well, go above the computer, thinking you are a more enlightened being.

The value of each crystal is has a dark secret, hence, it is hidden. And each new game, a new dark secret will come to light.
