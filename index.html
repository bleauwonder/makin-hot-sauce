Makin' Hot Sauce

How to Harvest

At the start of the game, the Computer will choose a random number.


There are 4 ingredients below. Each one is assigned a certain random number for the round and everytime you click, it will add to the total of your ingredients.


To win, match your total with the Computer's total. To lose, get just too overly exuberant about harvesting peppers.


The value of each ingredient has a dark secret, hence, it's value is hidden. And each new game, a new dark secret will come to harvest.
